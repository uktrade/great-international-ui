UK Investment Support Directory

  • Search for experienced advice

    The UK Investment Support Directory is a collection of companies that have skills and experience in helping overseas businesses set up or expand in the UK.

  • Find the right help

    To find a specialist, search the directory by entering the skill or service you are looking for in the search bar. You can also filter your search results by expertise in specific services, industry or by regional expertise.

  • Get in touch

    Contact your chosen specialist by clicking on ‘Contact company’ on their profile page. You will get your first hour of consultation for free and will also be connected to DIT for further support.

  • Join the directory

    Service providers operating in the UK can apply to join the directory. You must have the expertise and experience to help overseas business set up or expand in the UK. Contact us at investmentdirectory@trade.gov.uk to find out more.
